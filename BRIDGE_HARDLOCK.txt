WHAT "BRIDGE ROUTED (INTERNAL)" MEANS
================================================================================

"INTERNAL" refers to the ROUTING MECHANISM — the bridge is an internal part of
the DissOLinK system. It does NOT mean the destination is an internal page.

The destination can be:
  - link.me/[artist-slug]   (TYPE A — EXTERNAL destination, INTERNAL routing)
  - dissoverse.com/pages/go (TYPE B — INTERNAL destination, INTERNAL routing)

Both use the bridge. Both have the BRIDGE ROUTED comment. The destination is
determined by the link type (A vs B) — not by the comment.
